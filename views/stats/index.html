About MyJournal

MyJournal is a prototype framework of an existing journaling product I created for the United States Dressage Foundation. The Horse and Rider Together Program, fondly referred to as HART, allows horse riders to manage horse data, track riding details, and earn badges based on the number of hours spent in the saddle.

Since HART is aimed at a very specific group of users, I modified the prototype MyJournal to appeal to a greater audience and serve a more utilitarian purpose. I hope you enjoy it!
